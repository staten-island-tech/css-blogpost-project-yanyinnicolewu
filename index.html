My top 3 chinese cartoon movies

Nezha 2

A rebellious young boy, Ne Zha, is feared by the gods and born to mortal parents with wild, uncontrolled powers. Now that he's faced with an ancient force intent on destroying humanity, he must grow up to become the hero the world needs.

Wish Dragon

It tells the story about a college student named Din Song, who encounters a teapot inhabited by a dragon named Long, who has the powerto grant three wishes.

Jiang Ziya

A legendary warrior who defies the will of Heaven to follow his own path to righteousness. As a commander for the Celestial Army, he is tasked with executing a powerful nine-tailed fox demon but spares her life when he discovers an innocent girl's soul is bound to the creature. This act of defiance gets him banished from the heavens.
